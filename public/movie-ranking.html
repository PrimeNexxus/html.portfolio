The best Movies according to Meynard

My top 3 Movies of all time.

Samurai X

This is a story about a merciless samurai who have chance to have a change of a heart that there is more to life than being cold killer that has no remorse in life.

Spirited Away

The animation for this movie is superb and there are moral stories that should be learn from it.

The Boy and the Beast

This is a story of a young orphan who transported to the another world to meet the Beast who is not used to handle being with a person.
